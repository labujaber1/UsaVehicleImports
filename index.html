WELCOME to USA Vehicle Imports

We specialise in wholesale and import of pre-owned vehicles from East Coast USA. We provide a safe and secure way to import cars directly from the USA. We deliver your vehicle(s) from door to door using our own transport team. We have over 25 years’ experience in buying used American cars, RVs, convertibles, classics, motorbikes, and much more!
